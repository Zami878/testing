Hello, Codeforces!

We are pleased to invite you to IAEPC Preliminary Contest (Codeforces Round 999, Div. 1 + Div. 2).

The Preliminary Contest is a rated contest for Div. 1 + Div. 2, taking place on Monday, January 20, 2025 at 20:05UTC+5.5. The problems for this round were prepared by jqdai0815 and orzdevinwang.
